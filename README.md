Easily explain city finances using maps and graphs

## How to add a city
1. Here's the sheet for [Hurst](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/10XzX37RJKCnQho-a_yPE6meGILpF0mkTCbo5yz-XmF8/edit?usp=sharing) to model off of, duplicate for the city you want to add
1. Follow the strong towns guide for getting the basic financial data over time from the ACFRs
1. Gather the following data
1. Grab 2024 & 2025 revenues by property, sales, and hotel occupancy taxes
1. Property tax rates from FY 2015-2026. 2015-2025 can be found in the ACFR, 2026 will have to be grabbed from the latest budget
1. Population from US census. I get 1980-2020 from wikipedia, 2025 from US Census Quickfacts
1. Land area from wikipedia
1. Transpose data into "Formatted Data" tab so it can be put into a .csv
1. Get the city boundaries from overpass and export -> copy it into the geojson format
